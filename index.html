Projects

Zenflow - Automation of Routine Workflows

Developed a platform which enables automated production optimization opportunity identification and monthly production allocation for 450+ wells across the field. The platform was capable of suggesting opportunities like stimulation/wellbore cleanout, artificial lift optimization, etc by automatically fetching previous intervention reports and rates of each well, thereby saving human effort and time.

Zenith - Automated Field Monitoring System

Developed an automated field monitoring system which analyses all the (450+) well and field parameters and highlights the team about all major changes. A report gets automatically generated and sent via email in real time, working 24x7, contributing significantly to production optimization.

Reservoir Flooding Analysis

Developed a Web-based GUI for analysing breakthrough, saturation profiles and recovery plots for water flooding and polymer flooding in a reservoir. The saturation profiles for a particular flooding can be visualised as a function of time. Comparative analysis and visualisations of 'n' number of different flooding scenarios can also be done simultaneously.

Demulsifier Design

Explored and analysed various problems with the demulsification process that is carried out in the industry and further, with suitable research, went on to propose suitable solutions for the same. This was my B.Tech. (Petroleum Engineering), Final Year Project at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad.

Production Optimisation in HRP wells (INTERNSHIP PROJECT)

Developed a platform capable of analysing the past production data and dyna cards of the Hydraulic Rod Pumps (HRPs) in the wells, and predicting the optimum value of well parameters at which the oil production rates from the wells would be maximum. The actual implementation of these values were done on the field and actual rise in the production rates were observed. The platform was built in Python using Streamlit. Various concepts of Data Science (Machine Learning) were applied.

Wind Farm Simulator

The key problem of an unoptimised wind farm layout is the combined influence of wind turbines on the wind speed (Wake Effect). To tackle this, I started with designing an optimized layout for the placement of 50 turbines on a windfarm of a given area using various optimization techniques to obtain maximum annual energy production. Further, went on to generalise the idea for every possible field and any number of turbines to developed a web-based Wind Farm Simulator.

Choke Pressure Estimator

Applying the concepts of Choke Performance Relationship, build an independent standalone platform that could estimate the upstream/downstream pressure at the choke of the well. This was built in Python using PyQt5.
